2. Select Layer Cards

Drag cards to your weapon slots below. Each layer can only be used once.

Structure Layer - "What holds this together?"

Basic Frame
Low Risk 0 Slots

Holds all other layers together

Failure: Device bends, cracks, or collapses

Power Layer - "What makes this do anything?"

Manual Drive
Low Risk 1 Slot

Converts your effort into motion or force

Failure: Device locks up or kicks back
Stored Motion
Medium Risk 1 Slot

Releases stored kinetic force

Failure: Power releases all at once

Control Layer - "How does it decide what to do?"

Simple Trigger
Low Risk 1 Slot

Failure: Trigger sticks or misfires
Fixed Sequence
Medium Risk 1 Slot

Executes a set series of actions

Failure: Steps occur out of order

Sense Layer - "What can this detect?"

Physical Contact
Low Risk 1 Slot

Failure: Missed contact or false signals
Stress & Motion
Medium Risk 1 Slot

Failure: Overreaction or total silence

Safety Layer - "What prevents disaster?"

Emergency Release
Low Risk 1 Slot

Reduces damage when something goes wrong

Low Risk 1 Slot
